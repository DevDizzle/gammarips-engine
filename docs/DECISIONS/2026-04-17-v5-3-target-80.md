# Decision: V5.3 Target 80 — Pre-Defined Exits for Mobile Execution

**Date:** 2026-04-17
**Author:** eparra
**Status:** Implemented, audit pending, deploy pending
**Supersedes:** V5.2 "Ultra Simple" (never deployed — V5.3 is the live policy)

## Context

V5.2 (stop + timeout, no target) was designed and audited but not deployed. User reviewed Deep Research findings and elected to add the +80% target to capture the asymmetric profit-taking that Deep Research modeled as the primary EV contributor.

Deep Research direct quote on exits:
> "A pure 3-day timeout should be completely abandoned. Implement a hard take-profit target at +80% to +100%, combined with a trailing stop-loss that activates only after the option has achieved a +30% gain."

V5.3 adopts the hard target (+80%) and defers the trailing stop and underlying-based stop to Phase 2 — the latter two don't execute cleanly on Robinhood mobile for a hand-executed strategy.

User's operational constraint: trades executed from a phone. Pre-defined orders (stop + target GTC) at entry, manual timeout close at 3:50 PM day-3.

## Decision

Collapse to a single live strategy — **V5.3 Target 80** — with pre-defined exits:

### Execution (`forward-paper-trader`)
- **Entry:** 10:00 ET on day-1 (first trading day after `scan_date`)
- **Stop:** −60% on option premium (GTC stop-limit on Robinhood, paper models as option low piercing threshold)
- **Target:** +80% on option premium (GTC limit sell on Robinhood, paper models as option high piercing threshold)
- **Hold:** 3 trading days
- **Exit:** 15:50 ET on day-3 at market, or earlier if stop/target fires
- **Exit precedence on ambiguous bars:** STOP wins (conservative — we can't know intrabar sequencing)
- **Policy labels:** `policy_version = V5_3_TARGET_80`, `policy_gate = ENRICHMENT_ONLY_NO_TRADER_GATE`
- **Ledger:** continues writing to `forward_paper_ledger`. The 70 existing V4 rows (`V4_NO_GATE_SPREAD_ONLY`) remain as historical reference.

### Signal filtering (`signal-notifier`)
Unchanged from V5.2 plan. Layered on top of enrichment's `overnight_score >= 1 AND spread <= 10% AND directional UOA > $500k`:
- `volume_oi_ratio > 2.0`
- `moneyness_pct BETWEEN 0.05 AND 0.15`
- `VIX <= VIX3M` (fail-closed)
- `ORDER BY` directional UOA dollar volume, `LIMIT 1`

### Feature enrichment (`enrichment-trigger`)
Unchanged from V5.2 plan. Three NULLABLE columns on `overnight_signals_enriched`:
- `volume_oi_ratio FLOAT64`
- `moneyness_pct FLOAT64`
- `vix3m_at_enrich FLOAT64`

## What V5.3 does NOT include (Phase 2 backlog)

- 25% trailing stop activated at +30% (Deep Research recommended but not mobile-executable without OCO support)
- Underlying-based stop at opening range break (mobile execution can't condition on stock price for option stops)
- Sweep/block classification
- Aggressor-side (bid vs ask) detection
- GEX / dealer positioning signal
- Regime-conditional sizing

Each will be spec'd as a standalone decision note only after V5.3 accumulates 4+ weeks of paper EV evidence.

## Real-money posture

User elected to trade V5.3 with real money in parallel with paper ledger starting this week, at $500/trade, $2k starting bankroll. Max per-trade loss $300, max per-trade win $400. Paper continues as mechanical baseline for EV comparison against real P&L.

## Rollback plan

If V5.3 underperforms in paper + real over 4 weeks:
1. `git revert` the V5.3 implementation commit. Schema columns stay (harmless NULLs on old rows).
2. V4 and V5.3 rows coexist in `forward_paper_ledger` tagged by `policy_version` for clean analysis.
3. `scripts/research/` and `signals_labeled_v1` untouched per frozen-research policy.
4. User halts real-money trading immediately; paper continues until new policy replaces V5.3.

## Validation posture

- Paper + real parallel from deploy date. No further policy changes during first 4 weeks regardless of early P&L noise.
- If paper EV > 0 after 4 weeks → scale real size up.
- If paper EV < 0 after 4 weeks → pause real, reassess with fresh research pass.
- Direction mix is not a flaw — still reflects regime.
